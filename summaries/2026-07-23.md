**The Alphabet Aftermath: Your Next Move — July 23, 2026**

Owns Lockheed Martin (beat-and-raise quarter, likes it as a multi-year hold given missile/helicopter spending) and Cleveland Cliffs (beat estimates, trading like Micron — volatile but headed back toward $15 as steel prices and volumes rise).
