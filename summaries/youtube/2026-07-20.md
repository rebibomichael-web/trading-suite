### [Tesla's Biggest Advantage Keeps Growing](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EIAGqOk-0p4)
**Brighter with Herbert** · Jul 19

Tesla co-founder-adjacent investor Larry Goldberg fills in for Herbert this episode, and the two dig into Lucid's mounting troubles: reports say Lucid has hired restructuring firm Alex Partners amid bankruptcy speculation, and Goldberg argues the company's core problem was never technology (its battery/powertrain was genuinely strong) but founder Peter Rawlinson's ego-driven rivalry with Elon Musk and an inability to scale — Lucid sold fewer than 4,000 vehicles in Q2, and Goldberg says he'd short the stock and believes shareholders should exit now, calling its chances of profitability "vanishingly small." They also cover Tesla's new Brookshire, Texas Megapack factory near Houston, which will have ~50 GWh/year capacity (more than Lathrop and Shanghai combined) once ramped, building Megapack 3 (5 MWh, 28% more capacity than Megapack 2) plus integrated four-unit "Megablocks," with hiring targets of 375 employees by end of 2026 and 1,500 by end of 2028, and production starting late 2026. Finally, they discuss Boston Dynamics' Atlas robot performing at the World Cup and broader humanoid-robot progress, with Goldberg comparing robotics development to FSD — "easy to conceive, incredibly difficult to execute" — and praising Musk as an exceptional product manager while remaining early-stage skeptical on timelines for Tesla's Optimus and its new 10-million-square-foot robot factory in Texas.

### [Kaspa Lets Chat #10 Come hang out while I develop KaChat](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v919QyG9D7g)
**Kaspa Silver** · Jul 19

This video is a low-key livestream where Kaspa Silver (a Kaspa community developer) chats with viewers while building "KaChat," a private messaging app for the Kaspa (KAS) network. He announces KaChat 2.0 just went live on iOS with Android pending Google review, and previews KaChat 3.0 features he's actively building: a built-in portfolio tracker (to replace CoinMarketCap), an in-app swap feature via ChangeNOW, separate "spending" vs "chatting" wallet addresses for privacy, a redesigned profile/wallet management screen, dark/light mode, customizable menus, and cold-storage integration with the "Kasgner" airgapped signing device. He also floats a possible 3.5 update adding group chats and even on-chain chess. No price targets or trading calls are given — this is a project-development update, not market commentary, aside from his general bullish take that Kaspa's pure proof-of-work design and privacy-focused wallet architecture make it uniquely positioned versus other coins.
