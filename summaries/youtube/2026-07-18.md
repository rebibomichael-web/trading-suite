**Fundstrat** · Jul 17

Tom Lee of Fundstrat says the recent chip/semi/DRAM stock selloff (memory names especially) is a buying opportunity, not a warning sign, since AI infrastructure spending has years of runway ahead and these companies report earnings next week. He notes the pullback lets investors who missed the rally buy in roughly 20% cheaper, including Korean names.
