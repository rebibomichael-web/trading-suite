**Brighter with Herbert** · Jul 09

The hosts (filling in for Herbert) largely dismiss hype around a "$7.7k" or "77" announcement as a nothingburger, and speculate that new Palo Alto prototype-technician job listings likely point to Roadster or a Cybertruck-based "Cyber SUV" rather than an entirely new model.
